This website is about Sakura Trees

Sakura trees, or Cherry Blossoms, are a type of tree native to Asia. They are especially prominent in Japan.

Some random information.

Sakura has a vanilla-like smell.

The cherry blossom is the national flower of Japan.

Cherry leaves and blossoms contain coumarin.

The word sakura can refer to either the tree or its flowers.

In the presence of cherry blossoms, time stands still and the world becomes a sanctuary of serenity and grace.
